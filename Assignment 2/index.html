Storyline

Every thousand years a comet passes and determines the fate of earth, ridden with evil or embraced with peace. The first comet to appear, known as the Green Comet, housed the Lich, an evil being. The Green Comet led to the Great Mushroom War between the Lich and earth's inhabitants causing 1/3 of earth to be destroyed. One day Finn and Jake, a boy and a dog, are on an adventure and encounter the Lich. The two go on countless adventures in efforts subdue the threat with the help of some friends they made along the way. Will the land of Ooo know peace once more? Or will the Lich complete what he sought out to do?

Protagonists

Finn

A teenage human boy with a pure heart who enjoys fighting evil and helping others.

Jake

A dog who has magical stretching powers. He is easy going and carefree.

Ice King

An ice wizard who has had his sense of self taken away by the very crown he wears.

Marceline

A 1,000 year old half human half demon girl who very much resembles a vampire. She doesn't get along with her dad.

Princess Bubblegum

Creator and ruler of the Candy Kingdom.

Antagonists

The Lich

Emerged from the comet of desgruction who wants nothing more than to eradicate all life.

Gold

The most powerful being in the universe who creates all chaos.

Fern

A living grass version of Finn who came to be due to a curse.

Uncle Gumbald

A creation of Princess Bubblegum's who winds up getting jealous and turns against her.
